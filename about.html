About Me

Hello, and welcome. My name is Kaoru Murai (pronounced kuh-roo) and I’m a second year CS + Design student at the University of Michigan. My passion is connecting with others through the experiences I create with my code, design, and art.

I love pursuing the intersection between CS and Design — it is my belief that tech can push the creative boundaries of art, and add a whole new dimension to one’s experience. This is why my primary goal right now is to immerse myself in tech, UX/UI, design, and art to expand my creative expertise.

When I’m not coding or designing, you can find me elevating other creatives at the creator space I co-direct, dooting on my clarinet, creating animations or painting my nails.

If you’re interested in getting to know me better or have a dope project or collaboration in mind, please do not hesitate to hit me up !! Would absolutely love to meet you <3
