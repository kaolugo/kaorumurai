Hello, and welcome. My name is Kaoru Murai (pronounced kuh-roo, she/her) and I’m a third year CS + Design student at the University of Michigan. I'm currently set to work at frog design as a design technologist intern.

My passion lies in forming meaningful connections with others through the digital experiences I create, as well as forwarding the tech industry in my beautiful home country of Japan. I love pursuing the intersection between CS and Design — it is my belief that tech can push the creative boundaries of art, and add a whole new dimension to one’s experience.I care *a lot* about lowering the bar to pursue tech for underrepresented groups within the community and elevating those who want to learn.

When I’m not coding or designing, you can find me creating wholesome tech content on my YouTube channel, skateboarding across campus, traveling, taking self-timer photos or drinking boba.

If you’re interested in getting to know me better, want help or have a project or collaboration in mind, please do not hesitate to shoot me a message !! Would absolutely love to meet you <3
